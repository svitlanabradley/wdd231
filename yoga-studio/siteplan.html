Site Name

Yoga Time Studio

This name is easy to remember, aligns with the purpose of the website, and reflects the calming and rejuvenating experience that yoga provides.


Site Purpose

The purpose of Yoga Time Studio is to provide a welcoming and informative online space for individuals interested in yoga practice. The website will serve as a hub for potential students to explore available yoga classes, learn about their benefits, and easily connect with the instructor.


Scenarios

• What types of yoga classes are available, and which one is best for beginners?

• Where is the studio located, and is parking available?

• Is the studio eco-friendly and sustainable?
